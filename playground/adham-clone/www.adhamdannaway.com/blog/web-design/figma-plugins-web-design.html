10 best Figma plugins for web design in 2026

Top Figma plugins to help you design better websites, faster.

Adham Dannaway Last updated 1 January 2026

I’ve been using Figma for many years to design websites and applications. Although it’s a super powerful tool on its own, it can be made even more powerful with plugins that can extend its functionality.

In this article, we’ll explore some of the best Figma plugins for web design. I’ve found them super helpful in my design workflow. They can transform the way you approach web design, helping you create stunning, user-friendly websites with ease.

What are Figma plugins?

Figma plugins are powerful tools that extend the functionality of Figma. They’re created and maintained by third parties, basically other designers and developers. These plugins enable you to enhance your workflow by automating repetitive tasks, integrating with other tools, and adding new features that are not available in the core application.

Top Figma plugins for web design

Whether you’re looking to speed up repetitive tasks, add unique visual elements, or improve collaboration, Figma’s plugin ecosystem has something for everyone. It’s crazy how many plugins are available out there. If you can think of it, there’s probably a plugin that can do it. Let’s get into my top Figma plugins for web design.

Contrast

Quickly check WCAG colour contrast to ensure your web designs are accessible.

Contrast is one of the best Figma plugins for web designers focused on accessibility. It seamlessly integrates into your Figma workflow, allowing you to quickly check the contrast ratios of colours against the background according to WCAG standards. This immediate feedback facilitates on-the-fly adjustments, ensuring designs remain accessible throughout the design process. Additionally, Contrast can scan entire pages to identify text-based contrast issues, saving time and ensuring no element is overlooked.

The plugin’s versatility is another strong point, as it works on various elements, including text, gradients, image backgrounds, transparent fills, and more. The Smart Sampling feature accurately analyzes complex backgrounds, while the plugin’s speed and responsiveness make it feasible to keep open while working. 

Another great thing that sets this contrast checker apart from the others is that it supports the Advanced Perceptual Contrast Algorithm (APCA). This new and improved way to measure contrast is part of the WCAG 3 draft and it helps solve some of the limitations of the WCAG 2 method.

Contrast Figma plugin

Photopea

Edit photos, apply effects, filters, add text, crop or resize pictures inside Figma for free.

It’s crazy that the Photopea advanced photo editor plugin for Figma is free. It’s basically an Adobe Photoshop alternative inside Figma. By integrating Photopea’s robust photo manipulation tools directly into Figma, the plugin streamlines the editing process and eliminates the need to switch between applications.

It provides a web-based interface similar to Photoshop. Key features include layers with masking, cloning and healing tools, selection tools like Refine Edge, and a variety of image adjustment tools such as levels and curves. It also includes a RAW editor for basic photo adjustments, including exposure, temperature, tint, and contrast.

Photopea Figma plugin

Unsplash

Insert beautiful royalty-free images into your website designs.

The Unsplash plugin for Figma is a staple amongst designers seeking high-quality images to enhance their projects. By integrating Unsplash’s extensive library directly into Figma, the plugin simplifies the process of finding and inserting stunning, royalty-free images into your designs. 

This means that you can use images for personal or commercial use for free. Just make sure you filter images that have a free license. The intuitive search functionality allows you to quickly locate visuals that fit your design needs without interrupting your workflow.

Unsplash Figma plugin

Iconify

Get access to over 150 icons sets directly in Figma.

The Iconify plugin for Figma is really handy to get quick access to a wide range of icons. It integrates smoothly with Figma, allowing you to search for and insert vector icons from numerous libraries, including FontAwesome, Phosphor, Tabler, and Material Icons. This functionality simplifies the process of incorporating icons into your website or design system, saving you time and boosting your productivity.

You can also customize icons directly within Figma, making it easy to tailor them to fit your specific design needs. It’s even great if you’re looking for icon inspiration, as you can quickly search and compare a huge range of icon sets.

Iconify Figma plugin

Mesh Gradient

Generates a gradient image with an underlying 2d mesh, and lets you edit the vertices & edges of the mesh to get the perfect gradient look.

The Mesh Gradient plugin for Figma revolutionises the creation of gradient backgrounds, offering designers an intuitive and powerful tool to craft complex and visually appealing gradients. 

Unlike traditional linear or radial gradients, Mesh Gradient provides more control over colour transitions and blending, allowing for dynamic and intricate designs. Its user-friendly interface makes it accessible for designers of all skill levels, enabling you to manipulate colours directly on the mesh with ease.

This plugin significantly enhances creativity by opening up new possibilities for multi-color gradients, adding depth and visual interest to various design projects. Whether used for web and mobile interfaces, illustrations, or branding materials, Mesh Gradient’s versatility ensures it can meet a wide range of design needs. Additionally, its performance and responsiveness allow for real-time adjustments, ensuring a smooth and efficient workflow.

Mesh Gradient Figma plugin

Autoflow

Automatically draws arrows between frames in Figma to illustrate user flow diagrams in a hand-drawn or minimalist style.

The Autoflow plugin for Figma is a valuable tool for designers who frequently work with flow diagrams and wireframes. It simplifies the process of connecting elements by automating the creation of lines between selected objects. This functionality is especially useful for quickly mapping out user flows, organizational charts, or any other diagrammatic representation within your designs.

Autoflow’s intuitive approach allows for swift adjustments and updates, enhancing productivity by eliminating the need for manual line drawing and repositioning. Its ease of use and efficiency make it an essential addition to any designer’s toolkit, particularly for those who need to manage complex diagrams with minimal effort.

Autoflow Figma plugin

Mockuuups Studio

Automatically places your design into thousands of device mockups.

The Mockuuups Studio plugin for Figma is a popular tool for designers looking to enhance their presentations with realistic device mockups. It simplifies the process of showcasing designs by allowing you to quickly insert your work into a variety of device templates, including smartphones, tablets, and laptops. This streamlined approach saves time and ensures that mockups look polished and professional.

The plugin offers a range of customizable options, enabling you to tailor mockups to fit your specific needs. Its ease of use and integration with Figma make it a valuable asset for creating high-quality client previews and design presentations. Mockuuups Studio is great if you’re seeking to add a professional touch to your work with minimal effort.

Mockuuups Studio Figma plugin

Beautiful Shadows

Create beautiful shadows by dragging a light source which casts a shadow on selected elements.

The Beautiful Shadows plugin for Figma is a clever tool for designers who want to create polished and professional drop shadows with ease. It offers an array of customizable options, allowing users to fine-tune shadow properties such as blur, spread, color, and opacity to achieve the desired effect. This level of control ensures that shadows add depth and dimension to designs without compromising on quality.

The plugin’s intuitive interface makes it easy to apply and adjust shadows, saving time and effort in the design process. By simplifying the creation of beautiful, high-quality shadows, Beautiful Shadows enhances the visual appeal of your projects and helps maintain a consistent, professional look. A clever little tool for any designer aiming to elevate the aesthetics of their designs effortlessly.

Beautiful Shadows Figma plugin

LottieFiles

Quickly and easily animate your designs without leaving Figma.

The LottieFiles plugin for Figma is an essential tool for designers looking to bring their vector designs to life with animations. It streamlines the process of creating and exporting animations by allowing users to work directly within Figma and export their work to the Lottie format, which is widely used for its high-quality and lightweight animations.

With this plugin, you can preview your animations in real-time, ensuring that the final product meets your expectations before export. The integration between Figma and Lottie simplifies the workflow and enhances the efficiency of adding animations to designs. Overall, the LottieFiles plugin is a valuable addition for designers seeking to create engaging and dynamic animations with minimal hassle.

LottieFiles Figma plugin

Content Reel

Populate your designs with placeholder content including text, images, and icons.

The Content Reel plugin for Figma is really handy to populate your designs with placeholder content. It offers a convenient library of text, images, and icons that you can easily insert into your designs, making it particularly useful for prototyping and layout testing. You can also customize and save personal content collections.

With its user-friendly interface and versatile content options, Content Reel makes it simple to populate your designs with realistic placeholders, allowing you to focus on the overall design without getting bogged down by content management.

Content Reel Figma plugin

Incorporating the right plugins into your Figma workflow can significantly enhance your web design process. These are some of the best Figma plugins for web design and they offer a range of functionalities, from improving accessibility with tools like Contrast, to streamlining content management with Content Reel. Each plugin brings unique features that cater to different aspects of web design, helping you work more efficiently and creatively.

Whether you need to create stunning gradients with Mesh Gradient, quickly add high-quality images using Unsplash, or bring your designs to life with animations via LottieFiles, these plugins can transform your Figma experience. By integrating these tools into your workflow, you’ll be better equipped to tackle complex design challenges, save time, and produce professional, polished work.

Experiment with these plugins and discover which ones best suit your needs. As you integrate them into your workflow, you’ll likely find that your web design process becomes more streamlined, allowing you to focus on what you do best—creating beautiful, user-friendly websites.
